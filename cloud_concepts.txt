Cloud Service Models:
- IaaS: Provides virtualized computing resources over the internet.
- PaaS: Offers a platform allowing customers to develop, run, and manage apps.
- SaaS: Delivers software applications over the internet on a subscription basis.

Cloud Deployment Models:
- Public Cloud: Shared infrastructure available to the public.
- Private Cloud: Dedicated to a single organization.
- Hybrid Cloud: Combines public and private cloud environments.
